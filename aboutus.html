ABOUT OUR BRAND

The brand is aiming to create sustainable and inclusive clothing for every occasion. Originating out of the suburbs of
New York City, they took inspiration out of everyday wear from local New Yorkers. As other fashion brands shy away from using inclusivity
these guys pride themselves in using an inclusive models, sizes and staff. Everyone should be wearing clothes that they love.



ABOUT OUR DESIGNERS

The three childhood friends John, Adam and Chris all decided to get into fashion due to lack of inclusivity of designers.
They began their pursuit of creating a sustainable and inclusive clothing line in the backroom of their old job during the weekends.
After going viral on social media for posting their simple yet stylish clothing line, their business took off.
Five years later they have been featured in US Vogue and have a collaboration with deceased Virgil Abloh.
